# Sample Customer Questions & AI Responses

## Question 1

**Customer:**

How can I return a damaged product?

**AI Response:**

I'm sorry to hear that your product arrived damaged. Please keep the original packaging and contact customer support with your order details. If your purchase is eligible, you'll be guided through the return or replacement process.

---

## Question 2

**Customer:**

Can I cancel my order?

**AI Response:**

Order cancellation depends on the processing stage of your order. If it has not yet been shipped, cancellation may be possible. Please contact customer support as soon as possible for assistance.

---

## Question 3

**Customer:**

Do you provide warranty?

**AI Response:**

Warranty availability depends on the product. Please refer to the product documentation or contact customer support for product-specific warranty information.

---

## Question 4

**Customer:**

What payment methods do you accept?

**AI Response:**

Payment methods vary depending on the purchasing platform. Please check the checkout page or contact customer support for the latest accepted payment options.

## Question 5

**Customer:**

How do I contact customer support?

**AI Response:**

You can contact customer support through the official support channels provided by the company. If you require account-specific assistance, please have your order details ready.
